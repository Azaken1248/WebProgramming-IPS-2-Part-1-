HTML is really a very simple language.It consists of ordinary text, with commands are enclosed by “<” and “>” characters, or between an “&” and a “;” (for example <html>). You don't really need to know much HTML to create a page, because you can copy bits from pages that do what you want, then change the text! This page shows on the left as it appears in your browser, and the corresponding HTML code appears on the right. The HTML commands are linked to the explainations of what they do.
